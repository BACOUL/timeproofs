PRIVACY

Privacy Policy

Privacy-first by design. We process hashes only — never your content. No cookies. Edge-native security with HTTPS and HMAC signatures.

Effective date: 2025-10-22 • Last updated: 2025-10-22 • Version: v0.1

At a glance

  • No uploads: your files/content never leave your device.
  • Hash-only: we store a SHA-256 hash + timestamp + signature.
  • No cookies / trackers: none used.
  • Edge infrastructure: Cloudflare Workers + KV; TLS everywhere.

Data controller & contact

Controller: TimeProofs. Contact privacy: privacy@timeproofs.io • security: security@timeproofs.io • general: hello@timeproofs.io.

See also Legal Notice & Terms and security.txt.

Key definitions

  • Hash: irreversible SHA-256 digest (64-hex) of your content, computed client-side or by you on server.
  • Meta: optional, tiny JSON you send (e.g., {"type":"file","mime":"application/pdf"}) for categorization only — do not include personal data.
  • Proof: KV tuple: { hash, ts, sig, type?, meta? }.

Note: in context, a hash can constitute personal data if reasonably linkable to a person. Keep meta non-identifying.

Data we process

  • Hashes: provided by you; not reversible to original content.
  • Optional metadata: small fields you provide (meta).
  • Edge logs: minimal request metrics (route, timestamp, coarse IP region) kept ≤ 30 days on a rolling basis for security and reliability.

We do not collect names, emails, or user content via the public API.

How we use this data

  • Provide timestamping and public verification.
  • Prevent spam, fraud, and abuse; ensure reliability.
  • Plan capacity using aggregated, anonymous metrics.

Lawful basis (GDPR)

  • Performance of a contract/service: generate and serve proofs you request.
  • Legitimate interests: strictly necessary operational logging for security and reliability with limited retention.
  • Consent (if applicable): only for optional metadata you choose to send.

Your rights

Scope varies by jurisdiction. Because we store hashes and optional tiny metadata only, some rights may not apply or may require you to supply the exact hash.

  • Access / portability: request information we hold about a specific hash.
  • Rectification: not applicable to hashes; submit a new proof if content changes.
  • Erasure: request removal if optional meta accidentally included personal data.
  • Restriction / objection: you may object to non-essential processing; we minimize by design.

How to exercise

  • Email privacy@timeproofs.io with the hash concerned and proof of control (local re-hash or proof of possession).
  • Response times: 30 days (GDPR) / 45 days (CPRA), extendable where permitted.
  • We may request additional information to verify your identity.

Retention

Proofs are stored in Cloudflare KV for durability and public verifiability. Current policy: retain proofs indefinitely. Operational edge logs are short-lived (≤ 30 days) and purged on a rolling basis.

Security

  • TLS for all transport.
  • HMAC-SHA256 signatures over hash|ts with private secrets; keys rotated per policy.
  • Clock sync, minimal data design, rate limiting, and monitoring.

Report vulnerabilities to security@timeproofs.io or see security.txt.

Sub-processors

Cloudflare (Workers, KV, edge security, routing). Cloudflare may process limited operational metadata to deliver the service. No sale of data. No sharing for advertising. Changes to sub-processors will be reflected here or in Legal.

Cookies & tracking

No cookies, analytics beacons, or third-party trackers.

Do Not Track: honored by design; no tracking performed.

Global Privacy Control (GPC): respected. No sale or cross-site “sharing”.

US state privacy notices (e.g., CA/CPRA)

  • No sale or sharing: we do not sell or “share” personal information.

Opt-out & deletion requests

Email privacy@timeproofs.io with the hash, context, and justification. We will evaluate and, where appropriate, purge the entry from public verification. Deleting a proof removes public verifiability for that hash.

Children’s privacy

TimeProofs is a developer tool and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly process personal data of children.

Privacy-by-design

  • Zero-upload original content.
  • Hash-only, irreversible digests.
  • No cookies or trackers.
  • Edge-native, minimal state.
  • Open protocol and implementation.

Data protection summary

  • Processed: hash, ts, sig, optional meta
  • Not processed: original files, names, emails, behavioral data
  • Storage: Cloudflare KV
  • Security: TLS, HMAC-SHA256, key rotation, minimal logs
  • Retention: proofs = indefinite; operational logs = ≤ 30 days
