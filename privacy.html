Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 2025

1. Purpose

TimeProofs.io is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect or store personal data. Our system only handles cryptographic hashes — irreversible digital fingerprints that contain no user content or identity information.

2. Data Collected

  • Hashes: Only the SHA-256 hash you send is processed and timestamped. It cannot be reversed or linked to your content.
  • Metadata: Optional and minimal (type and meta fields) for technical categorization only.
  • Logs: Anonymous access logs (for abuse prevention) may be temporarily stored by Cloudflare.

3. How We Use Data

  • To provide proof-of-existence and timestamping services.
  • To prevent spam, fraud, or abuse.
  • To analyze anonymous usage statistics and ensure reliability.

4. Data Retention

Proofs (hash + timestamp + signature) are stored in Cloudflare KV indefinitely unless manually purged. No personal identifiers are ever stored.

5. Security

  • All communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Server-side signing uses HMAC-SHA256 with a private secret key.
  • No plaintext or user files are ever uploaded or stored.

6. Third-Party Services

We use Cloudflare for hosting and security. Cloudflare may collect anonymized metrics (like IP region or request counts) for performance optimization. No personal data is shared or sold.

7. Cookies

TimeProofs.io does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

8. Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected, no user identification or deletion process is necessary. For any concern, you can still contact us at privacy@timeproofs.io.

9. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated to reflect technical or legal changes. The “Effective date” will always indicate the latest version.
