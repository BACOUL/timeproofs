Privacy-first by design. We process hashes only. No content uploads. No cookies.

Effective: 2025-10-22 • Last updated: 2025-10-22 • Version: v0.1

At a glance

  • No uploads: your files never leave your device.
  • Hash-only: we store a SHA-256 hash, timestamp, signature.
  • No cookies / trackers: none used.

Data controller & contact

Controller: TimeProofs. Privacy: privacy@timeproofs.io • Security: security@timeproofs.io • General: hello@timeproofs.io.

See Legal and security.txt.

Key definitions

  • Hash: irreversible SHA-256 digest (64 hex) of content, computed client-side or by you server-side.
  • Meta: optional tiny JSON for categorization only. Do not include personal data.
  • Proof: object { hash, ts, sig, meta? } stored for public verification.

A hash may be personal data if linkable to a person. Keep meta non-identifying.

Data we process

  • Hashes you submit.
  • Optional metadata you provide as meta.
  • Edge logs with minimal metrics (route, UTC time, coarse IP region) retained ≤ 30 days.

We do not collect account data. We do not receive your original content.

How we use this data

  • Provide timestamping and public verification.
  • Prevent abuse and ensure reliability.
  • Plan capacity using aggregated metrics.

Lawful basis (GDPR)

  • Contract/service performance for requested proofs.
  • Legitimate interests for security and reliability with limited retention.
  • Consent for any optional metadata you choose to send.

Your rights

Some rights require the exact hash. We store no user account data.

  • Access / portability: request info about a specific hash.
  • Rectification: not applicable to hashes; submit a new proof if content changes.
  • Erasure: request removal if optional meta contains personal data.
  • Restriction / objection: you may object to non-essential processing.

How to exercise

  • Email privacy@timeproofs.io the concerned hash and proof of control.
  • Response: 30 days (GDPR) / 45 days (CPRA), extendable where permitted.

Retention

Proofs are stored for durability and public verifiability. Current policy: retain proofs indefinitely. Operational logs ≤ 30 days rolling.

Security

  • TLS for all transport.
  • HMAC-SHA256 over hash|ts with rotating secrets.
  • Clock sync, rate limiting, minimal data design.

Report vulnerabilities to security@timeproofs.io or see security.txt.

Sub-processors

Cloudflare (Workers, KV, edge security, routing). Processes limited operational metadata to deliver the service. No sale or advertising sharing. Changes will be reflected here or in Legal.

Cookies & tracking

No cookies. No analytics beacons. No third-party trackers.

DNT/GPC: honored. No sale or cross-site sharing.

EEA/UK addendum

  • Edge infrastructure may imply international transfers with appropriate safeguards.
  • You may lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

US state privacy notices

  • No sale or “sharing” of personal information.
  • Categories: hashes, optional metadata, minimal operational logs.
  • Requests: privacy@timeproofs.io.

Opt-out & deletion requests

Email privacy@timeproofs.io with the hash, context, and justification. If removed, public verification for that hash ceases.

Children’s privacy

TimeProofs targets developers and organizations. Not directed to children. We do not knowingly process children’s data.

Privacy-by-design

  • Zero-upload original content.
  • Hash-only, irreversible digests.
  • No cookies or trackers.
  • Edge-native, minimal state.
  • Open protocol and reference implementation.

Data protection summary

  • Processed: hash, ts, sig, optional meta
  • Not processed: original files, names, emails, behavioral data
  • Storage: Cloudflare KV
  • Security: TLS, HMAC-SHA256, key rotation, minimal logs
  • Retention: proofs = indefinite; ops logs = ≤ 30 days
