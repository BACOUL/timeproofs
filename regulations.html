1) Overview

TimeProofs is a privacy-first, stateless, interoperable proof-of-existence protocol and API. Hashing occurs locally; proofs are timestamped and publicly verifiable. No blockchain.

Goal: lawful, ethical, portable proofs across jurisdictions. No personal data required by default. Minimal logs.

3) Compliance by Design

  • Privacy-first: hashing is local; no uploads required.
  • Stateless API: independent requests; minimal logs.
  • Cryptography: HMAC-SHA256; Ed25519 planned (/.well-known/jwks.json).
  • Open verification: public ProofSpec, reproducible validation; offline verify planned.
  • Interoperability: portable .tproof.json bundles; JSON-LD metadata.
  • Anti lock-in: multi-backend drivers; BYOI; Docker.
