Regulations & Compliance

Global view. Updated Oct 27, 2025.

1) Overview

TimeProofs is a privacy-first, stateless, interoperable proof-of-existence protocol and API. Hashing occurs locally; proofs are timestamped and publicly verifiable without blockchain.

Design goal: lawful, ethical, portable proofs across jurisdictions. No personal data required by default. Minimal technical logs.

3) Compliance by Design

  • Privacy-first: hashing is local; no uploads required.
  • Stateless API: independent requests; minimal logs.
  • Cryptography: HMAC-SHA256 and Ed25519 (public keys via /.well-known/jwks.json), auditable timestamps.
  • Open verification: public ProofSpec, reproducible validation, offline verify planned.
  • Interoperability: portable .tproof.json bundles, JSON-LD metadata.
  • Anti lock-in: multi-backend drivers, BYOI, Docker.
