Security

Coordinated disclosure. PGP available. See also security.txt.

How to report a vulnerability

  • Email: security@timeproofs.io
  • Encrypt reports: /pgp.txt (PGP public key block)
  • Preferred languages: English, French

Please include affected component, steps to reproduce, impact, and a proof-of-concept if possible.

No legal action for good-faith research (see Safe Harbor)

Scope

In scope

  • API: https://timeproofs-api.jeason-bacoul.workers.dev/api and future api.timeproofs.io
  • Website: timeproofs.io and subpages
  • Protocol artifacts: Proof generation and verification logic

Out of scope

  • Denial of Service, volumetric traffic, or rate-limit bypass without user impact
  • Spam, social engineering, or physical attacks
  • Reports that rely on stolen credentials or non-supported browsers
  • Best-practice suggestions without a concrete security impact

Vulnerability classes of interest

  • Authentication / authorization flaws, key exposure, signature verification bypass
  • Crypto misuse around HMAC-SHA256, timestamp, or hash handling
  • Data validation resulting in meta injection or proof tampering
  • Server-side request forgery affecting verification endpoints
  • Cross-site scripting or CSRF on operator tools (if any)

Coordinated disclosure process

  • Acknowledge your report within 3 business days.
  • Triage and assign severity within 7 business days.
  • Remediate and deploy a fix as soon as feasible; we will share an ETA.
  • Credit with your name/handle on this page unless you prefer anonymity.

Safe harbor

We will not initiate legal action for good-faith security research that:

  • Accesses only what is necessary to demonstrate the vulnerability
  • Avoids privacy violations and data destruction
  • Respects rate limits and Terms
  • Gives us reasonable time to remediate before public disclosure

Rewards

No monetary bounties in v0.1. We offer public acknowledgments and early-access credits at our discretion.
