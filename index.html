How it works

From local hash to global proof

Three steps. All verifiable. Designed for privacy and portability.

1) Hash locally

Compute SHA‑256 in your app or browser. No data leaves the device.

2) Timestamp via API

Send only the hash. Receive a signed timestamp and a portable .tproof.json bundle.

3) Verify publicly

Anyone can verify the bundle offline or on /verify.html.
