FAQ

Why no blockchain?

We optimize for instant proofs, predictable cost, and privacy by default — no gas fees, no confirmation delays, no token lock-in.

What do you store?

Only the cryptographic fingerprint (SHA-256 hash) plus timestamp and server signature. Your content never leaves your device.
