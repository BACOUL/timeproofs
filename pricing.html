FAQ

What happens after beta?

Free plan remains with reasonable limits. Pro unlocks higher quotas, PDF certificates, webhooks, and a dashboard. You can upgrade or cancel anytime.

Do you store my files?

No. We only store the SHA-256 hash plus the timestamp, signature, and optional minimal metadata. Your content never leaves your device.

Is there a per-request fee or gas?

No blockchain, no gas. We aim for transparent, predictable pricing based on monthly volume.

Do I need an API key?

Not during beta for public endpoints. Pro introduces API keys and per-project quotas.
