FAQ

What happens if I exceed my Beta quota?

During public beta, soft rate-limits may apply per IP. For sustained higher volume, request Pro early access so we can provision API keys and quotas.

Do you store my data?

No. We only store the hash you send (64-hex) alongside the timestamp and signature. Your original files never leave your device. See our Privacy Policy.

How are proofs verified?

Public read-only endpoint /verify?hash=… returns existence, timestamp, and signature (HMAC-SHA256 over hash + timestamp). Anyone can verify without accessing your content.

Will pricing change?

We target predictable, no-gas pricing suitable for automation and AI pipelines. Exact quotas and price points may adjust at GA based on usage and costs.

Payments?

Stripe for subscriptions & overages at GA. For pilots or enterprise, contact us.
